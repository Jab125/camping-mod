# Simply Camping

## Added features:

 - Pine and dense pine forests, the perfect places to camp!
 - Natural hot springs
 - Sap that can be scraped off of pine trees
 - Cloth from ferns
 - Cheaper campfires that heal you
 - A tent for warmth at night
 - Sleeping bags, a portable way to sleep without setting your spawn point
 - Marshmallows, that you can cook around the fire
 - S'mores, that you can craft with marshmallows
 - Bears that you can find in pine forests, don't get too close!
 - Bass, a new type of fish
 - Improved fishing, actually pull in the fish

## Upcoming features:

 - Squirrels
 - Backpacks to store your camping items
